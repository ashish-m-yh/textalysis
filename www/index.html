Get Started

High customer volumes require that organizations pay closer attention to dissatisfied customers. With increasing scale, it becomes harder to identify customer retention opportunities purely through manual processes.

This is how Textalysis offers a window into your customer data and provides actionable reporting.

  • Voice of the Customer

    • Identify customers' pain-points and highlight cases which need to be addressed urgently to ensure best chances of customer retention.

  • Management Visibility

    • Managers would have greater visibility into performance of each service executive based on satisfaction levels of customer accounts they manage. Customer issues of highest severity could be escalated too.
